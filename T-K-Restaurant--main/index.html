Our Story

Welcome

As two fathers who are passionated about food, Our goal is to provide an experience that bridges our two cultures (Nigerian-Americans) and provide a legacy to not just our children but the entire melting pot that is the Portland metro area.

Our mission is simple: to celebrate our culture and bring an unique gastronomic experience that incorporates fresh ingredients and flavors originating our native Nigeria and West african delicacies.
